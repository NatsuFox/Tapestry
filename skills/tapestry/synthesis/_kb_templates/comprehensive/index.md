# Knowledge Base

## Purpose

This is the top-level index for the book-like knowledge base built from Tapestry feeds.

It should remain the coarsest semantic map of the full collection.

## Topics

- [AI And Research](ai-and-research/index.md)
  machine learning, AI systems, research papers, and technically substantive AI commentary
- [Markets And Trading](markets-and-trading/index.md)
  trading systems, market structure, signal evaluation, and execution psychology
- [People And Profiles](people-and-profiles/index.md)
  researchers, creators, authors, and account-level context
- [Community QA And Discussion](community-qa-and-discussion/index.md)
  multi-voice discussions, social threads, QA ecosystems, and comment-driven knowledge
- [General Reference](general-reference/index.md)
  useful but weakly clustered material that does not yet justify a stronger topic

## Maintenance rule

Whenever a new topic is created or an existing topic is split, update this file first.
